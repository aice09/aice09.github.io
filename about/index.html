About

I am Carl Angelo Pamparo Nievarez from the Queen City of Bicol. I graduated from Central Bicol State University of Agriculture - Sipocot Campus with a degree in Information Technology last 2016. I am currently the Web Developer and IT of the MIS department of Kou Fu Color Printing Corporation for more than 3 years.

I have fun working with the Information Technology field and still now, I don’t know what things I need to master to this path. Most of the time I work with server configuration and explore different operating systems, firewalls, proxy, databases,storage servers,hypervisors and other projects. I also work with our internal web-based system and collaborate with other programmers from other countries.

I also love working with Adobe, Corel, Autodesk, and other CAD and rendering applications. I’ve also been studying ethical hacking and penetration testing using Kali Linux, and Parrot. For my free time I’ve always watched tech tutorials and talked about audio and video conferencing setups with my friends and played DOTA 2. And I also work as a freelancer if I have some customers to work with.

In addition, I also create youtube tutorials, write blogs at dev.to, answer different questions in stack exchange, and participate in different application forums.

For the future I’ve been interested in taking Google, Microsoft, and Cisco certifications. Implement DevOps culture in my company. And looking forward to establishing a tech company with my friends.
